Today we had a special visit from the Mass General Hospital’s BloodMobile at our office building location in Watertown, MA. A number of us at ClinLab Staffing were happy to sign up! The event was hosted by Paradigm Properties who owns the Riverworks building where our Massachusetts office is located. Our neighbors at InCrowd did a great job organizing the event!

According to the MGH Blood Donation Center one blood donation can help save up to three lives and less than 4% of the eligible population actually donates blood.

We look forward to supporting more events like this in the future.

We have been very impressed by Molly May who continues to be a leader in our community outreach efforts. Molly has brought Team ClinLab together to participate multiple events this year organized by Life Science Cares, an organization of Life Science professionals who come together to reduce the impact of poverty on our neighbors in the greater Boston area.

For a special Mother’s Day event Life Science Cares has partnered with Women in Bio, WEST, NEWISE, and North Shore Technology Council to collect critical items such as diapers and underwear for mothers in need in the community. Today concludes the Mother’s Day Drive which appears to have been very successful. We have been honored to participate under the leadership and coordination of Molly.

Happy Mother’s Day!
